Job Opening: Senior Software Engineer


Job Duties

  1. Write product or system development code.
  2. Participate in, or lead design reviews with peers and stakeholders to decide amongst available technologies.
  3. Review code developed by other developers and provide feedback to ensure best practices (e.g., style guidelines, checking code in, accuracy, testability, and efficiency).
  4. Contribute to existing documentation or educational content and adapt content based on product/program updates and user feedback.
  5. Triage product or system issues and debug/track/resolve by analyzing the sources of issues and the impact on hardware, network, or service operations and quality.
  6. Staying on top of technology trends and developments.

Job requirements

Must have at least 5 years’ experience in
  • Familiar with Golang language
  • OOP or Functional Programing and modeling skills
  • IN depth knowledge of Linux: CentOS, Rocky Linux，Debian, etc.
  • Extensive performance tuning experience with MySQL
  • Extensive experience using and tuning CockroachDB
  • Experience with CI/CD
  • Proficiency with docker and Kubernetes
  • Experience working in an agile environment
  • Experience implementing and consuming large-scale web services
  • Experience with Automating Testing and deployment pipelines using devops tools such as Jenkins.
  • Fluent with functional, imperative and object-oriented languages; knowledge of Golang, Java, C, C# or JavaScript would be especially useful

Employer will accept any suitable combination of education, training or experience. This should be read to mean that the employer requires: Bachelor Degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or relevant with 5 years of experience in the job offered, technical project manager, technical director or Equivalent.
